About Me

It all started when I was young, that I was able to travel the world and live in many different places. I became an adventure seeker, a foodie and a travel expert. In the past few years I have been flying for free and on first class, all thanks to credit card points. I started this back in 2012 and with the ongoing devaluations of points, it has become more challenging.


With all the travel and adventures I have done, I wanted to blog and document my life. Now that I am programming, I am able to expand on my website and create new content.
